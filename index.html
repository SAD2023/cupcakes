About Us!

In 2003, Tiffini had an idea of creating a cupcake only bakery! Then there was no such thing as a cupcake only bakery and the idea sounded very wild and crazy. But why was it common to find a bakery full of cakes in many flavors but only a few with cupcakes in many flavors. Why not give people cupcakes, in lots of delicious flavors to choose from, every day. In March 2004, Yummy Cupcakes went into the cupcake business and has grown now to over 400 cupcake flavors with selections in vegan, gluten-less and sugar-free plus an exciting selection of specialty Cupcake treats!
